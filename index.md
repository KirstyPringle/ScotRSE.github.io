# The Scottish Research Software Engineers (ScotRSE) Group

These pages provide a hub for existing Scottish RSE groups, motivate new groups to form in Scottish Institutions and an opportunity to network between these groups or an individuals if there are no local groups.

## Stay in touch!

If you would like to hear about the ScotRSE network activities please join our mailing list:  SCOTRSE at JISCMAIL.AC.UK and check out the #Scotland channel on the UKRSE Slack area. 

## Organisations in Scotland with RSE Groups

* [Community of Edinburgh RSEs (CERSE)](https://cerse.github.io)
* [Univeristy of Edinburgh - EPCC](https://www.epcc.ed.ac.uk)
* [St Andrews RSE group](https://rse.cs.st-andrews.ac.uk/)
* [Univerisity of Strathclyde](https://www.strath.ac.uk/science/computerinformationsciences/hackyhour/)
* [James Hutton Institute](https://www.hutton.ac.uk/)

If you know of a Scottish RSE group not on the list please let us know!
